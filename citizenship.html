What is Citizenship? What are the necessary componets of Citizenship?

Citizenship is the act of being a good citizen. In order to be a good citizen you need to be a good person That means showing respect, having a good attitude or just helping out. The-Signal

  • Honesty: Free from fraud or deception : legitimate, truthful Merriam Webster. Honesty is necessary component of good citizenship because honest is one of the most important things to be, it builds trust, its respectful, helps cause less problems, and can make finding a solution easier.
  • Compassion: Sympathetic consciousness of others' distress together with a desire to alleviate it Merriam Webster. Compassion is a necessary component of good citizenship because, you need to be able to help and be there for one another.
  • Respect: An act of giving particular attention, high or special regard. Merriam Webster. Respect is a necessary component of citizenship because In order for people to trust you, and like you, and to be a good person you have to have respect and be respectful towards others. You have to show you can listen and help.
  • Responsibility: The quality or state of being responsible, able to choose for oneself between right and wrong. Merriam Webster Responsibility is a necessary component of citizenship because, people need to be able to trust you and you need to be able to make right decisions and do what is right. You need to be able to do whatever you need to, to help and solve problems and do what is right for people and yourself.
  • Courage: Mental or moral strength to venture, persevere, and withstand danger, fear, or difficulty. Merriam Webster. Courage is a necessary component of citizenship because, you need to be able stand up for what you believe in and stand up for others, you need to be able to have a voice and not afraid to do what is right.
  • Kindness: The quality or state of being kind. Merriam Webster. Kindness is a necessary component of citizenship because, you need to be nice to people and be friendly in order to be a good person and for people to like you.
